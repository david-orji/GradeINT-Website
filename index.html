The Challenge

Connectivity shouldn't dictate testing quality.

Traditional computer-based testing systems expect flawless infrastructure. When the internet drops, systems freeze, student progress is lost, and institutional integrity is compromised. GradeINT completely eliminates this vulnerability by bringing the intelligence to the edge, guaranteeing that tests continue without interruption.

A resilient exam flow.

Four simple steps to absolute reliability.

01

Configure & Download

Administrators create the exam and download the encrypted package to a local server or facilitator machine.

02

Local Distribution

The exam is distributed over a local network to candidate devices. No external internet is required.

03

Take the Exam

Candidates complete their test. Responses are encrypted and saved locally in real-time, preventing any data loss.

04

Sync & Grade

When connectivity is restored, results securely sync to the cloud for immediate AI grading and reporting.
